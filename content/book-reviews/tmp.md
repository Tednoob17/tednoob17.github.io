In this section, I share reviews of books I use to learn and improve in infosec.

There will be two review formats:
- Some books will be covered through multiple chapters/posts to go deeper step by step.
- Others will be treated in a single post with a fast global summary.

Most of the books come from publishers like **No Starch Press**, **O'Reilly**, or **Packt Publishing**.

I personally started infosec by reading [Phrack](https://phrack.org) and solving [Root-Me](https://www.root-me.org/) challenges, and I believe books are still one of the best ways to build strong fundamentals.
