Art
===

Cette page sert de carnet visuel: d'un côté les photos prises avec un appareil, de l'autre les dessins. L'idée est d'avoir une structure simple, lisible, et facile à remplir au fil du temps.

## Artists I follow

<div class="art-links">
*   [Steeven Salvat](https://www.steeven-salvat.com/2024) - Selected artworks from 2023.
*   [Michelle Carlos](https://www.michellecarlos.com/) - Illustrations inspired by her dreams, travel, childhood memories and musings about the world around.
*   [Smex (Alex Weir)](https://smexart.com/) - Artist & illustrator based in Glasgow. His practice explores the significance of everyday life, celebrating ideas of community, connection and identity.
*   [Seth Clark](https://www.sethsclark.com/) - Pittsburgh collage artist and sculptor.
*   [Momentum Gallery](https://momentumgallery.com/artists/) - A contemporary, modern program, with an emphasis on emerging and mid-career artists in a downtown Asheville art gallery.
*   [June Digan](https://www.junedigann.com/) - Designer and illustrator based in Manila. Her work focuses on strong emotions and storytelling using delicate characters, whimsical landscapes, and lettering.
*   [Nick Sheehy](https://www.nicksheehy.com/) - Art & Illustration / Showchicken.
*   [Shelby Dawn Smith](https://www.shelbydawnsmith.com/) - Emerging Canadian artist focusing on colour, mark-making, texture, and shape.
*   [Amber Ma](https://www.amberma.com/) - Illustration & Visual Storyteller.
*   [Talia Janover](https://www.taliajanover.com/) - Multi-disciplinary Designer & researcher, specializing in interactive installation design.
*   [Beth Mitchell Fine Art](https://bethmitchellfineart.com.au/) - Award winning Australian Fine Art Photographer who specialises in Underwater Photography.
*   [Jessie Bearden](https://www.jessiebearden.com/) - Multidisciplinary artist and creative director living in Los Angeles.
*   [Steven Kozar](https://stevenkozar.com/) - American Hyper-Realist Artist.
*   [Redd Walitzki](https://www.reddwalitzki.com/) - A leading New-Contemporary artist whose ethereal oil paintings explore the ephemeral beauty of nature and humanity.
*   [Nada Frikha ](https://graphiste.com/nada_frikha) - Freelance graphic designer and illustrator specializing in clean, modern visual identities and branding.
*   [Gawx Film](https://www.gawxfilm.com/) - Cinematography and filmmaking portfolio showcasing high-energy visual storytelling and creative video production.
*   [Gawx World](https://gawxworld.com/) - Creative hub and lifestyle brand featuring vibrant "doodle" art style, merchandise, and multi-disciplinary projects.
</div>
